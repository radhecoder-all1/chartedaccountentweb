Frequently Asked Questions

What makes CAPRO different from other CA firms?

CAPRO combines 30+ years of traditional accounting expertise with cutting-edge technology solutions. Our client-centric approach means we assign a dedicated account manager to each client, ensuring personalized service and deep understanding of your business needs.

How do you ensure the security of my financial data?

We use bank-grade encryption for all data transmission and storage. Our systems are ISO 27001 certified, and we implement strict access controls. Regular security audits and employee training ensure your data remains completely confidential.

What is your typical response time for client queries?

We guarantee a response within 4 business hours for all urgent matters. Most routine queries are addressed within 24 hours. Our client portal also provides 24/7 access to your financial documents and status updates.

Do you offer services for startups and small businesses?

Absolutely! We have specialized packages for startups and SMEs that include company registration, accounting setup, tax planning, and compliance management at affordable rates. We understand the unique challenges faced by growing businesses.

How are your fees structured?

We offer transparent pricing with three options: fixed-fee packages for standard services, hourly rates for specialized work, and retainer models for ongoing comprehensive support. During our initial consultation, we'll recommend the most cost-effective structure for your needs.
